## Tips for Using with Gemini

1. **Be Specific**: Include exact hex colors
2. **Reference Style**: Mention "modern", "premium", "clean"
3. **Request Variations**: Ask for 2-3 options
4. **Specify Dimensions**: Mention "desktop view" or "mobile responsive"
5. **Include Context**: "For a marketing campaign platform"
6. **Ask for Exports**: "Provide high-resolution mockup suitable for development"
