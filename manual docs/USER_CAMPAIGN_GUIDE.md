## üé® 2. The Campaign Builder

Create stunning, high-converting landing pages in minutes without any coding.

### A. Hero Section (The First Immpression)
This is what visitors see first. Make it count.
*   **Headline:** Write a catchy hook (e.g., "Spin & Win Daily Prizes!").
*   **Subheadline:** Explain the value (e.g., "Free shipping, discounts, and more.").
*   **Background Image:** Upload a high-quality image that represents your brand.

### B. The Spin Wheel üé°
The heart of the engagement.
*   **Customize Segments:** Define 6-8 slices.
    *   *Example:* "10% Off", "Free Burger", "Better Luck Next Time", "Mystery Prize".
*   **Win Probability:** Control how often each prize is won. You have full control over the odds.
*   **Colors:** Match the wheel to your brand colors using the visual picker.

### C. Social Tasks (Viral Growth) üöÄ
Don't just give away freebies‚Äîget something in return! Require users to complete tasks to earn more spins.
*   **Instagram Follow:** Grow your follower count.
*   **Facebook Share:** Get your campaign in front of their friends.
*   **Verification:** Our system checks if they actually clicked the link before awarding spins.
*   **Cooldown Timer:** A built-in delay ensures users stay on the target page long enough.

### D. Offer Showcase
Display your products, services, or vouchers below the wheel.
*   **Card Layout:** Beautiful grid display of your offers.
*   **Details:** Add images, titles, descriptions, and "Claim" buttons.
*   **Categories:** Organize offers by type (e.g., "Food", "Drink", "Merch").
*   **Direct Links:** Send users directly to your checkout or booking page.

### E. Footer & Trust
Add credibility to your page.
*   **Contact Info:** Phone, Email, Address.
*   **Social Links:** Icons linking to your main profiles.
*   **Legal Pages:** Terms of Service, Privacy Policy (essential for running ads).

## üí∏ 5. Maximizing Profit: The Secret Sauce

Turn fun spins into serious revenue.

1.  **The "Loss Leader" Strategy:**
    *   Give away something small (free appetizer, free shipping) to get them in the door.
    *   *Result:* Most customers will buy a main course or a full-priced item once they are there.

2.  **The "Upsell" Coupon:**
    *   Instead of "Free Product", offer "‚Çπ500 Off on orders above ‚Çπ2000".
    *   *Result:* You increase your Average Order Value (AOV). The customer spends *more* to use the coupon.

3.  **Remarketing Goldmine:**
    *   You now have their phone number. Don't let it sit there!
    *   *Action:* Send a WhatsApp blast on Friday: "Hey! You still haven't used your 10% off spin prize. Expires tonight!"

4.  **Create Urgency:**
    *   Add a "Spin expires in 24 hours" note in your campaign description.
    *   *Result:* Drives immediate action instead of "I'll do it later."
